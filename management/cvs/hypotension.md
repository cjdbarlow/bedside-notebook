###Vasopressor Non-Response
Potential causes of haemodynamic resistance to vasopressors include:
* Preload
	* Occult bleeding
* Inotropy
	* Acidosis
	* Hypocalcaemia
	* Hypothyroidism
	* Toxins
		* Î²-blockade
		* Calcium channel blockers
		* Digoxin
	* Hypothermia
* Afterload
	* Anaphylaxis
	* Adrenal insufficiency
